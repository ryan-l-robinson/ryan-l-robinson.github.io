Meet Ryan

Ryan is a web developer, active in his Mennonite church, commuter cyclist, and a proud cat person.

Technology

Ryan works as a Drupal web developer. He has a Bachelor of Computing Honours with a specialization in Cognitive Science (B.CMP.H, Queen's University, 2010). Other technologies he has worked with include:

  • GitLab, including project management tools and CI/CD scripts
  • Docker and VS Code developer environments
  • Microsoft 365: Power Automate, SharePoint, Teams
  • WordPress
  • CiviCRM
  • eBook publishing

Some of the results of Ryan's technology work, as well as some occasional unrelated technology notes, are avaialble on Ryan's technology blog.

Church

Ryan is active with Rockway Mennonite Church in Kitchener, Ontario. He holds a Master of Divinity degree (M.Div., Queen's University 2012) and some of his activities with the church include:

  • Managing the audio system or video live stream during worship gatherings
  • Coordinating schedules and training for the tech volunteers
  • Serving as a member of the Worship Committee, planning and overseeing the vision for the Sunday morning gatherings
  • Occasionally preaching, and previously serving on the Preaching Committee
  • Leading discussions in Adult Education hour

Some of the results of these activities, as well as some occasional unrelated musings, are recorded on Ryan's theology blog.
